Hydrogen Atom AR Experience

Scan the target image to visualize a hydrogen atom with orbiting electron in augmented reality

How to Use This AR Experience

1

Prepare the Target Image

Print the target image or display it on another device. Make sure it's clearly visible.

2

Start the AR Experience

Click the "Start AR Experience" button below to activate your camera.

3

Scan the Image

Point your device's camera at the target image. Make sure the entire image is visible in your camera view.

4

Explore the Atom

Once detected, you'll see a hydrogen atom appear above the target image. Move around to view it from different angles.
